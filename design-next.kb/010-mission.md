# Mission

Give one operator — and every agent that drops into their 10–20
parallel sessions a day — a single coherent system for three jobs that
today are spread across nine organically grown skills:

1. **Durable knowledge**: what we know, believe, and have decided,
   queryable at token-efficient granularity.
2. **Attention**: what work exists, what it's worth, and what has
   gone stale — across sessions, repos, and life domains.
3. **Convention enforcement**: the rules that must hold, holding by
   mechanism rather than by reminding the model harder.

The v1 ecosystem proved the core bets (filesystem as database, small
homogeneous files, schema-validated frontmatter, dated records) and
disproved one: that prose emphasis can enforce behavior. It also
predates the 2026 platform primitives — hooks that inject context,
path-gated rules and skills, plugins as a distribution unit — that
now do natively what v1 hand-rolled.

Success looks like: an agent orients in under a minute; conventions
hold without IMPERATIVE blocks; a fix to shared behavior lands in
exactly one place; the operator stops finding half-landed renames.
