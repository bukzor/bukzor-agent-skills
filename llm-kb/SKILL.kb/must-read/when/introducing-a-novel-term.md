# When Introducing a Novel Term

You're about to name a term, concept, or thing by a short alias for the
first time in any kb content file. Examples: a failure-mode alias, a
principle's short name, a domain noun the reader might not know.

**Stub the concept or glossary entry at first mention.** See
`../../procedures.kb/stub-missing-entry.md` for the procedure.

## Why this is a hard step, not soft advice

Deferred stubbing produces cross-references that don't resolve, which
produces readers who don't follow them, which produces alias drift
across the kb. Each missed stub is a debugging cost paid by the next
agent.
