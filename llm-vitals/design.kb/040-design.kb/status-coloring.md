# Status Coloring (Red / Yellow / Green)

Imported from EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System) Scorecards.
Each vital surfaces a status indicator beyond raw debt numbers:

- **Green**: tier 1 healthy AND tier 2 within budget
- **Yellow**: tier 1 healthy AND tier 2 within budget but burn-rate
  projects breach (leading indicator firing)
- **Red**: tier 1 breached OR tier 2 over budget

Color is the primary at-a-glance signal in the dashboard; raw debt
numbers are available on drill-down. This is a legibility design:
twelve vitals × debt-in-days is a wall of numbers; twelve vitals ×
color is parseable at a glance.

Coloring also makes the dashboard suitable for ambient surfaces
(daily digest email, mobile widget) where rich numbers are
impractical.
