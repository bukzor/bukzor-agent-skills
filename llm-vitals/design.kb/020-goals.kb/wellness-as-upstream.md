# Wellness as Upstream of Work

A significant fraction of confusion and inefficiency is downstream of
poor wellness: under-slept decision-making, neglected relationships
producing emotional drag, lack of restoration leading to brittle
attention. Treating wellness as separate from work is a category
error; wellness *is* the substrate of work effectiveness.

The system treats wellness domains (body, relationships, restoration,
meaning) as first-class vitals on equal footing with work vitals.
Wellness debt is rendered visible by the same mechanism that surfaces
revenue debt, so wellness slippage becomes early-warning data rather
than an end-of-quarter realization.
