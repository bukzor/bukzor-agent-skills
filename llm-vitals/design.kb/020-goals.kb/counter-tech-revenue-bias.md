# Counter Tech / Revenue Bias

The operator's natural energy gradient pulls toward "nice technical
problems" — well-defined, controllable, intrinsically rewarding — and
away from revenue-generating work, which is often messier, externally
gated, and emotionally heavier.

Left unchecked, this bias produces months of pleasing technical
output and runway depletion in parallel.

The system counters this by making revenue-debt **visible as a single
number** at every session start (e.g., "days since revenue work: 12")
and biasing the picker to surface revenue work first when debt exceeds
a threshold. The mechanism is non-coercive: it makes the imbalance
impossible to ignore, not impossible to choose against.
