# Grow Incrementally

The system must be useful from day one with minimum infrastructure,
and accumulate signal over time that unlocks richer features. A big
upfront investment in tracking would fail because the data to
calibrate it doesn't exist yet.

Specifically:

- Tier 1 (check-in cadence) works immediately with no data
- Tier 2 (metric thresholds) layers on once tier 1 is reliably healthy
  for that vital
- Calibration features (burn rate, rabbit-hole scoring) require
  accumulated history

The system is **quality-gated, not time-gated**: each layer adds when
its prerequisites are met, per-vital, not on a calendar.
