# Wheel of Life Visualization

A radial chart showing each vital as a spoke, with debt/status
rendered as length or color. Direct analog of the coaching tool of
the same name.

Useful when sharing the dashboard with a non-technical audience —
the radial form communicates imbalance at a glance, no SRE
vocabulary required.

Deferred because: the operator's daily use of the dashboard is fine
with a tabular CLI view. Visualization is for sharing, not for
operating. Add when there's a person to share it with.
