# Multi-Axis Surface

The system tracks multiple independent vitals simultaneously and
presents them as a single coherent surface. No single ranking
collapses across axes; each vital reports its own status and debt.

Three independent traditions converged on this requirement: Balanced
Scorecard (business), Wheel of Life (wellness coaching), and Golden
Signals (SRE). The pattern is well-validated: a single
priority-ranked list cannot represent a domain where rotting is
independent per category.

Implies: the picker shows ~12 axes at once (the working-memory limit),
each with its own status. The visualization is multi-dimensional
(dashboard, not list).
